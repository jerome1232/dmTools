Welcome to _Dev_Null_'s DM Tools!

Our tools are designed to help Dungeon Masters like you create and run exciting and immersive campaigns for your players. We offer a variety of resources that are customizable and easy to use. All of our tools are released under the GNU General Public License version 3(GPLv3), which means that they are free and open-source. You can use, modify, and distribute them as you see fit.

Currently this only includes:
  • Loot Generator - Generate randomized treasure for your players based on encounter difficulty. You can choose to generate treasure based on individual monsters or for a hoard's cache. Loot generation is entirely based on loot tables found in the DMG.
Current plans are to add:
  • Encounter Combat Tracker - Track initiative order, status effects, AC, HP, and other stats.
  • Name Generator - Generate unique and memorable names for NPCs, locations, and magical items. Choose from a variety of categories and customize the generator to suit your campaign.

Suggestions and feedback are welcome!

Our tools are available on GitHub at jerome1232/dmTools. We encourage you to contribute to the project and make it even better for the DM community.
